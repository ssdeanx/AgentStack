# Product Guidelines

## Brand Voice and Tone
Our communication follows a balanced approach to ensure clarity, trust, and ease of use:
- **Professional & Authoritative:** We provide expert-level insights and maintain high standards of accuracy and reliability.
- **Friendly & Approachable:** We use conversational and warm language to make complex AI concepts accessible to everyone.
- **Minimal & Modern:** We value brevity and sleek design, focusing on utility and removing unnecessary noise.

## Visual Identity
- **Clean Layouts:** Prioritize whitespace and clear hierarchy.
- **Modern Typography:** Use legible, contemporary sans-serif fonts.
- **Functional Color Palette:** Use colors to indicate status and action without overwhelming the user.

## Writing Style
- Use active voice.
- Avoid excessive jargon; explain technical terms when necessary.
- Keep instructions concise and actionable.
